ABOUT US

Pop Smart Kids started with the goal of creating digital playgrounds for kids that promote creativity and connectivity to others. Our vision is to create educational apps that reach a broad spectrum of kids with different learning goals. In doing so, we also want our apps to be a safe zone for kids and their families so they don't have to worry about anything while using our apps. We started the company back in 2017.
